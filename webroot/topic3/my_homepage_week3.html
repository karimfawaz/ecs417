About Me

Passionate software developer. Currently working on my mobile development skills along with some game development and E-commerce websites.
For as long as I can remember, I have loved finding efficient solutions to everyday problems to optimise the way we live. I have always been intrigued by economic, political and environmental issues and tried to suggest solutions while discussing them with senior family members. As a young man, my solutions weren't the most efficient, but I listened keenly to their remarks to revolutionise the way I think but never gave up on my beliefs. Later, I was able to fix minor technological bugs my family faced, which made me 'The Tech Guy' amongst family members and friends. Through this, I discovered my interest in programming and the new thinking dimensions it offers and knew I had to master it in order to transmit my solutions to the world. I have always known that my future lied in Technology.

My Project: Brain 2.0

Brain 2.0 started out as a simple idea in my head, in 2017. I wanted to build games and apps that solve problems on a daily basis. I wanted to build projects with efficiency as its main goal. Ever since then I have taught myself how to code using C# to build some UNITY 2D games, I have created over 12 2D games, 2 of them were published in 2018. I then taught myself how to code in Python to learn how to automate tasks, solve math problems, and learn the basics of web-scraping. I have used Java and Kotlin on Android Studio to build basic Android Apps (6 unpublished projects). I am currently developing 4 Brain 2.0 Projects using Flutter. I have already published (On the PlayStore) an application to help out my local barber shop during my country’s worst economic crisis by including their contact information, haircut example pictures, location, and appointment scheduling system, in a themed mobile application. I have recently built a productivity application to introduce people to a few tips and tricks to take control over their lives, such as the 80/20 rule, the pomodoro timer’s purpose, tracking their procrastination, easily waking up early, building routines and much more.
